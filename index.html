Frequently asked questions

How long does website deployment take?

Most static websites are live within 3-5 business days after you provide your content and branding assets. Complex customizations may take longer, and we'll communicate timelines upfront.

Can I keep my existing domain?

Absolutely! We can help you transfer your existing domain to our management or simply configure DNS settings to work with our services. No downtime involved.

Do you offer monthly billing options?

Yes! While we list yearly prices for best value, most services can be billed monthly upon request. Contact us for a custom monthly payment plan.

What happens after I request a quote?

We'll respond within 24 hours with a detailed proposal, pricing breakdown, and timeline. No obligation to proceed — we're happy to answer questions first.

Do you support businesses outside South Africa?

Currently we focus on South African SMEs, but many of our digital services (websites, chatbots, email) work globally. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

What's your cancellation policy?

Monthly services can be cancelled with 30 days' notice. Yearly services are non-refundable after 14 days, but you retain access for the full paid period.
